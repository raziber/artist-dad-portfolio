About

Udi Berg

Udi Berg works across sculpture, painting, ceramics, blacksmithing, and drawing. His practice is guided by material: clay, metal, pigment, line, heat, weight, and the physical intelligence of making by hand.

Whether the work is forged, fired, painted, or drawn, it carries the same sensibility — tactile, elemental, and disciplined, with equal attention to force, surface, and presence.

“The work carries force, but it also carries touch.”
