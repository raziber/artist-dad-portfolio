About Me

I work across sculpture, painting, ceramics, blacksmithing, and drawing. My practice is guided by material: clay, metal, pigment, line, heat, weight, and the physical intelligence of making by hand.

Whether the work is forged, fired, painted, or drawn, I return to the same concerns — touch, force, surface, and presence.

“The work carries force, but it also carries touch.”
